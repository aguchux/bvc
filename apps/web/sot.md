1. Institution Overview
   About Bonny Vocational Centre

Bonny Vocational Centre (BVC) is a vocational and innovation enterprise institution established in 2004 by Nigeria LNG Limited (NLNG) in partnership with the Bonny Kingdom. The Centre was founded to address middle-level technical manpower needs, promote entrepreneurship, and empower youths from NLNG host and Gas Transmission System (GTS) communities.

BVC delivers industry-aligned technical, vocational, and professional training, combining practical skills development with internationally recognised certifications.

Core mandate:

Technical skills for sustainable development

Workforce readiness and employability

Entrepreneurship and innovation

Community capacity building
